Millionaire Trivia Game

This is a simple game that resembles the popular tv show "Who wants to be a millionaire".
Please click on the button bellow to figure out how to play.

×

Click on the button "Start game" to start your quest on becoming a millionaire!
Firstly, proceed to read carefully through the question. You have to choose only 1 correct answer. In order to do that simply click above the selected answer - a blue background will appear to assure you that you have chosen one. If you hesitate between 2 answers you may choose to use any of the lifelines:

50/50 -- will eliminate 2 wrong answers
Phone-A-Friend -- will give you suggestion by a friend
Public -- will ask "the public" for the correct answer


Then if your answer is correct you will proceed to the next one and if it is not - your game will be over. You may restart it any time by clicking on the "Restart" button.
Enjoy!
